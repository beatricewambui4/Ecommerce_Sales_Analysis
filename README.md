# 🛒 USA Ecommerce Sales Analysis

## Project Overview
An end-to-end ecommerce sales analysis project using MySQL 
for data storage and querying, and Power BI for visualization 
and business intelligence reporting.

## Business Problem
As a data analyst for an ecommerce business, the manager 
needed a clear view of performance across revenue, profit, 
customers, regions and products to make strategic decisions 
for 2026 and 2027.

## Dataset
- Source : USA Ecommerce Sales Data (2011–2014)
- Records: 9,994 rows
- Fields : 21 columns including Orders, Customers, 
           Products, Sales, Profit, Discount

## Tools Used
| Tool      | Purpose                        |
|-----------|--------------------------------|
| MySQL 8.0 | Data storage & SQL analysis    |
| Power BI  | Dashboard & visualizations     |
| Power Query| Data transformation           |
| DAX       | Calculated measures            |
| PowerPoint| Project documentation          |

### Page 2 — Growth & Strategy
- YOY Revenue & Profit Growth
- Monthly Revenue Trend (Seasonality)
- Top 10 Customers by Profit
- Discount Impact on Profitability

## Key Insights
1. Total revenue of $2.3M with 12.47% profit margin
2. Technology is top category at $0.84M (36.6%)
3. New York City leads cities at $256K revenue
4. Revenue growing at 20.62% YOY
5. 1,318 orders (26%) are loss-making
6. Average 20% discount is compressing profits
7. Q4 (Oct-Dec) is peak season consistently

## Recommendations for 2026
1. Cap discounts at 20% maximum
2. Promote First Class shipping (highest margin 13.93%)
3. Focus marketing on New York and Los Angeles
4. Launch Q1 promotions to boost slow Jan-Feb period
5. Expand Technology product range
6. Develop Corporate segment with account management
